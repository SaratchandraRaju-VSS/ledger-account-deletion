Ledger App – Account & Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your Ledger App account and permanently remove your personal data, you can request it anytime.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Your deletion request will permanently remove:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • Your Google Sign-In data (name, email, UID, profile photo if stored)
  • All stored data associated with your account

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email to:
support@ledgerapp.app

Include the following:

  • Your registered Google email address
  • Statement confirming deletion request
  • Optional: screenshot of your profile

Processing Time

Your request will be processed within 7 days. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
